Independent Mechatronics Project: Soft Robotic Hand

In collaboration with a friend, I created a low-cost silicone hand model embedded with nylon wire with joints that are adjusted using servo motors tuned by a rotary encoder. The data acquired from the rotary encoder is sent to a silicone mold hand model embedded with nylon wiring that is adjusted via tendon driven actuation.
